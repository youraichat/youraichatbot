PREMARKET HIGHLIGTS 🔎 
 

💲 CPI Expectations
Economists' median forecasts say CPI inflation likely grew 4.0% on an annual basis last month and 0.1% on a month-over-month basis. Core CPI is expected to grow by 5.3% annually and 0.4% MoM.

If May's inflation numbers outpace expectations, we could see a very negative reaction in today's trading session. 

What to Watch Today 👀 
 

May CPI Report

Last month's consumer price index data will drop at 8:30 a.m. today, and the report will probably have a significant influence on the market. Investors are hopeful that the Fed is nearing the end of its rate hike path, but, unless there's a policy change, the central bank will continue raising rates until inflation falls below its 2.0% target. Today's CPI report will tell us if inflation is still going in the right direction.
